U.S. College Campus Safety and Security Report

Campus Safety and Secuirty is an important issue for students, parents and school administrators. In order to provide data on this subject, The Department of Education release annual reports on campus-related crimes and incidents. The following two visulizations aim to illustrate a number of different types of incidents which occurred from 2012 to 2014. The data is from the Campus Safety and Security site.
